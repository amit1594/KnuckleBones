Welcome to Six-Six



66 is a strategy board game with a bit of luck involved.

Each player, on their turn, rolls a dice at places it on one of his three columns.
Columns can hold up to 3 dice.
Each Column's value is calculated separately, according to the following rules:

  • If a column has 2 of the same dice, it gains that dice times 4 as value.
  • If a column has 3 of the same dice, it gains that dice times 9 as value.

The game stops when a player fills their entire board, meaning he has 9 dice.
Then, the winner is the player with the most amount of points.
I.e. you can be the last player to place a dice and still lose.
